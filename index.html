Angkor Wat - Siem Reap

Available -10%

Angkor Wat is one of the most magnificent ancient temples in the world and the largest religious monument on Earth. It was built in the 12th century by King Suryavarman II as a dedication to the Hindu god Vishnu. With its stunning stone carvings and grand architecture, the temple showcases the brilliance of Khmer civilization. Millions of visitors travel here each year to admire its beauty and peaceful surroundings. The sunrise at Angkor Wat is one of the most iconic and breathtaking sights in the world. The temple is also a powerful national symbol of Cambodia, appearing proudly on the country's flag. Wandering through the temple grounds feels like stepping back in time, full of history and sacred atmosphere.
